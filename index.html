Welcome to Heldorya

Step into the majestic realm of Heldorya, where valiant adventurers gather beneath the towering walls of Valdareth Castle. Forge your destiny among ancient stones, inhabited villages, and magical mysteries.

The grand view of Valdareth Castle, heart of Heldorya.

Laws of the Land

  • Respect thy fellow players; no harassment or griefing.
  • Building is forbidden in the spawn area; acquire or rent houses to dwell.
  • Players causing damage to others may be confined to the castle's underground cells as punishment.
  • Donations and pay-to-win mechanics are not allowed; the realm remains fair to all.
  • All adventurers must seek prior permission before joining to ensure harmony and mod compatibility.

Explore the World

Venture beyond the castle walls to discover a world enriched by wondrous mods, vibrant biomes, and magical creatures.

  • Build your own homestead in the purchasable or rentable houses near the spawn village.
  • Traverse the enchanted Arcane Labyrinth, a maze imbued with ancient magic and secrets.
  • Trade and barter at the bustling Mart or the trusted Bank, where treasures change hands.
  • Harvest and cook exotic ingredients in the villagers' specialized shops, enhanced by culinary mods.
  • Mount steeds and beasts from the castle stables, your loyal companions on every journey.
  • Gather with kin in the Church or share tales in the Throne Hall.
